How to get best results

For optimal results, please make sure your image complies with the following guidelines:


  • Photograph is portrait

  • One face on the photo
  • Face unobstrcuted and clearly visible
  • Eyes open and clearly visible
  • Transparent glasses are ok, but no flares
  • No dark glasses
  • Neutral or medium emotion
  • Face angles:
  • Yaw: +/- 30 degrees
  • Pitch: +/- 15 degrees
  • Roll: +/- 45 degrees
  • Image size (pixels between the eyes):
  • Minimal: 30px
  • Recommended: 100px

What is Best Fit Me?

Best Fit Me is a personality profiling and behaviour prediction algorithm based on machine learning and computer vision.

In other words, it's an AI image recognition model that can predict your psychological chracteristics based off an image of your face.

How do I use it?

The Best Fit Me personality test is the easiest personality test out there! Simply upload an image of your face and our algorithm will do the rest.

Is it secure?

When you upload an image to Best Fit Me, it goes straight to the API hosted on a remote server for processing. The server then returns a package of data which is what we show to you. At no point during the process are any of your images stored anywhere.
